The rise and fall from DoKwon

Our story began somewhere in the slums of South Korea. A poor man named Do Kwon was sitting in a run-down shack, struggling to survive. He had no money, no friends, and no connections. He was truly a poor villain of the lowest social class. One day he was about to commit suicide, but the devil came to his rescue. He said, "Do Kwon, you can become rich and famous, but you must take my place as the Devil." Do Kwon didn't hesitate and agreed. Thereupon, he directly made the most evil plan the world had ever seen. Do Kwon founded Terra and created the Luna coin. He played the smart visionary and thus found investors who financed his project. All this for his diabolical plan. He quickly made a lot of money and bought a new house, sports cars and expensive watches. A few years have passed and Do Kwon sits in his Terra Tower surrounded by his subordinate Luna followers, brainwashed for so long that they celebrate him like a K-pop star. There was only one brave man who saw the horrible thing coming. It was Algodtrading, one of our future superheroes in this saga. He had challenged Do Kwon to a bet of one million dollars,

but Do kwon quite arrogantly remarked, "your size is not size."

Then the most successful trader in the crypto field GCR stepped in, who brought the required "size". He was willing to bet $10 million with Do Kwon."

At this point, Do Kwon realized that the time had come to put the final phase of his plan into action. The Crash! The Luna and UST coins dropped to the bottomless pit, and the Terra network collapsed. Since Do Kwon was the devil, he feasted on the suffering of investors who lost entire livelihoods through him and partly committed suicide. Knowing that people would hunt him down, he took all the money and fled to the protection of darkness. But where there is shadow, there is also light. And so the biggest and bravest men rose up to form an alliance and come to the aid of the crypto community. Our superheroes were born. The hunt begins...
